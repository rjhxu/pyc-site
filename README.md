## 🎨 About Platform for Youth Creativity

Platform for Youth Creativity (PYC) is all about shining a massive spotlight on young entrepreneurs, artists, and small businesses! 🚀 We love creating vibrant spaces and hosting super fun events to celebrate, uplift, and connect youth creativity within our community. 

Our mission? To give young creators the ultimate stage to shine, share their amazing work, and vibe with fellow youth in the arts community. Feel free to take a look around! 🌟
